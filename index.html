Crystals Collector


Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crysal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to a random number. You lose if your total score exceed the random number.
The value of each crytal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
